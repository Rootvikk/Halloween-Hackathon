The History of Halloween

Origins of Halloween

Halloween originated from the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain, where people lit bonfires and wore costumes to ward off ghosts.

Why Do We Celebrate Halloween Today?

Today, Halloween is a celebration of fun, costumes, and community events, with children and adults alike participating in festivities.

The Tradition of Costumes

Wearing costumes on Halloween started as a way to disguise oneself from spirits, a tradition that has since evolved into creative, fun costume parties.
